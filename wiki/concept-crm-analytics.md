# CRM Analytics

## What It Is
CRM (Customer Relationship Management) analytics = using data from client interactions, deal history, and engagement activity to understand client health, predict churn, and identify growth opportunities.

## Common CRM Platforms
- **Salesforce** — enterprise standard, highly customizable
- **HubSpot** — popular with mid-size companies, strong reporting built-in
- **Pipedrive** — sales-focused, simpler
- **Notion / Airtable** — lightweight, common at smaller agencies

## Key Metrics

### Pipeline Metrics
| Metric | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Pipeline Value** | Total $ value of all open deals |
| **Win Rate** | Deals won / total deals closed |
| **Average Deal Size** | Average $ value of won deals |
| **Sales Cycle Length** | Avg days from first contact to close |
| **Pipeline Coverage** | Pipeline value / revenue target (target: 3–4x) |

### Client Health Metrics
| Metric | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Renewal Rate** | % of clients who renew contracts |
| **Expansion Revenue** | Additional revenue from existing clients (upsells) |
| **Churn Rate** | % of clients lost in a period |
| **NPS / CSAT** | Client satisfaction scores |
| **Engagement Score** | Frequency/depth of client interactions |

## Common Analysis the BI Analyst Runs
1. **Client segmentation** — tier clients by revenue, margin, growth potential
2. **Churn prediction** — identify at-risk clients based on engagement signals
3. **Upsell opportunity mapping** — which clients use only one LDS service but could use more?
4. **Pipeline health reporting** — is the sales team on track to hit revenue targets?
5. **Win/loss analysis** — why are we winning or losing deals?

## Relevance to LDS Role
JD: "Own client data files and CRM management, developing scalable processes to track client behavior, engagement trends, and commercial activity."

This is an ownership role — not just pulling reports, but building and maintaining the CRM data infrastructure. At a company of ~80 people, the BI Analyst likely IS the CRM admin and analyst combined.
