Client diversity is high — studios, sports federations, consumer brands, podcast networks. Each client type has different revenue models (ad share vs. licensing vs. retainer). The CRM and client analytics work involves tracking very different engagement patterns across these segments. Renewal/expansion analysis (ZDF, Cineverse) is a real workflow.
