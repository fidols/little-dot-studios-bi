# Little Dot Studios

## Overview
Little Dot Studios (LDS) is a multi-platform production company, social media agency, and digital media network founded in 2013. Headquartered in the UK with offices in the US (Los Angeles), Germany, and ANZ. Part of All3Media group. ~80 employees. Verified carbon neutral since inception, targeting net-zero by 2030.

## Products & Capabilities

### Social Media Agency
- World's leading YouTube specialists for entertainment, sports, and consumer brands
- Services: strategy, audience development, content production, monetization, digital copyright management

### Production Company
- Long-form broadcast-quality film and TV
- Award-winning podcast series
- Short-form reactive social content and live streams

### Digital Media Network — Little Dot Channels
- 135+ channels across social platforms
- 6 FAST channels on streaming services
- 21,000+ hours of licensed long-form premium content
- 125 million+ unique viewers monthly
- Large global premium advertising inventory across YouTube and CTV

### Specialist Divisions
- **WING** — sport and branded content production agency
- **History Hit** — Dan Snow's podcast network and SVOD service

## Clients
Works with major TV and movie studios, distributors, rights holders, sports federations, and brands. Specific clients not publicly disclosed in detail.

## Business Model
Revenue streams include:
- Agency fees (social media management, YouTube channel management)
- Production fees (content creation)
- Ad revenue sharing (YouTube monetization, CTV advertising)
- Licensing fees (content distribution)
- Subscription revenue (History Hit SVOD)

## Relevance to BI Role
The Commercial Planning team sits at the intersection of all these business lines. The BI Analyst role supports Finance, Operations, and Commercial leaders — meaning the data spans project time tracking, client revenue, staffing costs, and channel performance.
