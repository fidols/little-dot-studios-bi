# Source: Little Dot Studios — Revenue, Scale & Business Model

**Sources:** littledotstudios.com, Digiday, ZoomInfo
**Ingested:** 2026-05-12

## Key Facts

- **Revenue:** $257.7M
- **Employees:** ~419 across 4 continents (as of March 2026)
- **Scale:** 1,000+ digital channels globally, 11 billion organic organic views/month
- **Content ID:** Helped partners earn $100M+ in passive revenue over 3 years by managing content rights on YouTube

## Revenue Streams (confirmed)
1. Platform and direct ad sales
2. Brand-funded content and commercial partnerships
3. Passive revenue from digital copyright management (Content ID)
4. Content licensing fees
5. Subscription (History Hit SVOD)
