# Source: All3Media — Parent Company Financials

**Sources:** Variety, Deadline, Broadcast Now, Yahoo Finance
**Ingested:** 2026-05-12

## What is All3Media?
All3Media is a British television, film, and digital production and distribution company — LDS's parent. Known for producing *The Traitors*, *Fleabag*, and other major shows. Acquired by RedBird IMI in May 2024.

## Key Financials (2024)
| Metric | 2024 | 2023 | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| Revenue | £895.9M ($1.19B) | £995.2M ($1.33B) | -10% |
| EBITDA | £105.3M | £107.4M | -2% |
| Operating result | -£30.8M loss | +£43M profit | flipped to loss |
| Pre-tax loss | -£113.5M | -£27.8M | worsened |

## Revenue by Region (2024)
- **UK programme revenue:** £297.1M (down 16% from £355.2M)
- **US revenue:** £314.8M (down 11% from £352.7M)
- **Rest of world:** £284M (down slightly)

## Context
- Revenue drop reflects first full year under new ownership (RedBird IMI acquired May 2024) + challenging market conditions
- 2025 outlook: All3Media forecasting revenue growth and EBITDA above £105M
- **Banijay merger:** All3Media is in the process of merging with Banijay Entertainment. Banijay reported revenue up 3.4% to ~€4.9B for 2025 and called it a "monumental week" when the merger closed.

## What This Means for LDS
All3Media had a tough 2024 — revenue down £100M, flipped to operating loss. This creates **cost pressure** throughout the group, including LDS. For the BI Analyst role, this context is important:
- The Commercial Planning team will be under pressure to demonstrate ROI and improve margins
- Finance partnership is not just administrative — it's strategic during a period of parent company stress
- US expansion (new Co-MDs, LA BI role) signals LDS is investing in growth while the parent recoups
- The Banijay merger could bring structural changes — worth monitoring
