**Sources:** winglondon.com, deadline.com, variety.com, rccdbcars.com
**Ingested:** 2026-05-12

## What WING Does
WING (acquired by LDS in March 2020 for its first major acquisition) is a sport and branded content production agency founded in 2007 by Will and Tessa Ingham. Will Ingham is now LDS's Chief Creative Officer (promoted Feb 2026).

WING specializes in:
- High-end film and photography for sports brands
- Fast-turnaround social content tied to live events (race calendar, Olympic schedule)
- Branded content that integrates multiple sponsors into single campaigns
- Strategic distribution across YouTube, Instagram, and social platforms

## McLaren
WING produced brand content, behind-the-scenes footage, and sponsor activations for McLaren. McLaren is the most-followed F1 team on social media — agencies like WING are a core reason why.

**Notable work:**
- "Driven to Deliver" — viral branded video featuring McLaren drivers delivering Amazon parcels and Coca-Cola Zero bottles. First step of a multi-brand collaboration with merchandise drops and signed item giveaways.
- High-end film and photography for team and sponsor content

**What makes McLaren content operationally complex:**
- Multiple concurrent sponsors with different reporting requirements
- Content must align with race calendar (tight deadlines 23x per season)
- Behind-the-scenes access requires on-site production crew at races

## Formula 1 (Mercedes-AMG Petronas)
- Produced **23 Race Previews** for Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 team in a single season
- Fast-turnaround content: 23 deadlines in ~9 months, each requiring film, edit, and multi-platform distribution
- Operationally intense — peak resourcing demand aligned with race weekends

## Olympics (IOC)
- Produced **Olympic Torch Relay content for Rio 2016**
- Worked on **Tokyo Olympics** coverage
- Massive global productions requiring significant crew, travel, and tight project timelines

## America's Cup, Vodafone, Land Rover, Hugo Boss, Coca-Cola
Sports and consumer brand content — film, photography, social campaigns.

## BI Relevance: Why This Work Is Hard to Manage Financially
WING's project portfolio is operationally complex for a BI Analyst to track:

| Challenge | Implication |
|---|---|
| Race calendar = 23 fixed deadlines | Budget burn rate must be tracked per race, not just per season |
| Multi-sponsor activations | Revenue attribution across clients on shared projects |
| On-site production (travel) | Variable direct costs that are hard to estimate upfront |
| Olympic/event surges | Capacity spikes — need freelancer planning months in advance |
| Fast-turnaround content | Less time for scope creep prevention — overruns happen fast |

This is exactly the type of project portfolio where time tracking analytics, utilization monitoring, and budget vs. actual reporting deliver the most value.
