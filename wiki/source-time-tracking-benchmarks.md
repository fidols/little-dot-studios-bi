# Source: Time Tracking Tools & Utilization Benchmarks

**Sources:** getharvest.com, toggl.com, SPI Research 2025
**Ingested:** 2026-05-12

## Top Tools for Agencies

### Harvest
- All-in-one: time tracking + invoicing + expense tracking + project budgeting + team scheduling
- Best for: professional services businesses that need to track time AND manage project budgets and profitability in one place
- Strong reporting: budget vs. actuals, billable hours, team utilization

### Toggl Track
- Simple, minimal — one-click time tracking
- Best for: teams that just need clean time data, with integrations to other tools for budgeting/invoicing
- Lower friction = better adoption across teams

### Clockify
- Free tier available
- Popular at smaller agencies

## Utilization Rate Benchmarks

| Role | Target Utilization |
|---|---|
| Delivery / production roles | 75–90% |
| Project managers | 50–65% |
| Agency average (all roles) | 65–75% |
| Industry average (most agencies actually run at) | 55–60% |

**SPI Research 2025 Benchmark:** 75–80% is the established target for billable utilization in delivery roles.

## What Low Utilization Costs
If a 10-person team runs at 55% instead of 75%, that's 20% of capacity going unbilled. At $125/hr average billing rate, that's ~$260K/year in lost revenue per 10 people.

## Interview Application
> "I'd start by auditing utilization by role — are production staff hitting 75%+ or are we closer to the 55% industry average? A 10pp improvement in utilization on a team of 20 is meaningful revenue without adding headcount."
